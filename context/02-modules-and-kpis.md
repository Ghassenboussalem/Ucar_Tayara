# Modules & KPIs Deep Dive

## 15 Core Modules

---

### 1. Scolarité (Enrollment)
**KPIs:**
- Total enrolled students per institution
- Enrollment rate vs capacity
- New enrollments vs previous year (delta %)
- Dropout rate at enrollment stage
- Gender ratio, regional origin breakdown

**Features:**
- Digital enrollment forms with OCR for document upload
- Automated eligibility validation
- Waitlist management

---

### 2. Examens (Exams)
**KPIs:**
- Success rate per subject / per semester / per institution
- Failure rate by subject (flag outliers)
- Exam attendance rate
- Grade distribution (bell curve analysis)
- Retake rate

**Features:**
- Exam schedule management
- Results ingestion (manual + API)
- Anomaly detection on grade distributions

---

### 3. Pédagogie (Pedagogy)
**KPIs:**
- Number of active programs (filières)
- Accreditation status per program (ABET, EUR-ACE, ISO 21001)
- LMS adoption rate
- Teaching load per faculty member (vs 400h threshold)
- Adjunct faculty ratio (vacataires %)
- Student satisfaction score per program

**Features:**
- Program catalog management
- Accreditation deadline tracker
- LMS integration (Moodle, etc.)

---

### 4. Finance
**KPIs:**
- Budget allocated vs consumed (% execution)
- Cost per student
- Spending by department
- Revenue sources breakdown (tuition, grants, partnerships)
- Budget deviation alerts (>10% threshold)

**Features:**
- Budget entry and tracking
- Automated variance alerts
- PDF/Excel financial reports

---

### 5. Ressources Humaines (HR)
**KPIs:**
- Total headcount (teaching vs administrative)
- Absenteeism rate
- Training hours completed per staff
- Teaching load distribution
- Vacancy rate
- Staff turnover rate

**Features:**
- Staff directory and profiles
- Leave and absence tracking
- Training completion tracking

---

### 6. Recherche (Research)
**KPIs:**
- Number of publications (indexed: Scopus, WoS)
- Active research projects
- External funding secured (DT)
- Patents filed
- PhD students enrolled
- International research partnerships

**Features:**
- Publication registry
- Project tracking with milestones
- Funding pipeline management

---

### 7. Partenariats (Partnerships)
**KPIs:**
- Active national agreements
- Active international agreements
- Incoming student mobility (Erasmus+, etc.)
- Outgoing student mobility
- Joint degree programs count
- Partnership renewal rate

**Features:**
- Agreement database with expiry alerts
- Mobility tracking per student
- Partnership impact scoring

---

### 8. Vie Estudiantine (Student Life)
**KPIs:**
- Active student clubs count
- Student participation rate in activities
- Scholarship recipients (% of total students)
- Scholarship processing time (days)
- Residence occupancy rate
- Daily meals served
- Psychological support consultations
- Harassment/incident reports (active cases)

**Features:**
- Club management portal
- Scholarship application workflow
- Residence management
- Event calendar

---

### 9. Infrastructure
**KPIs:**
- Total campus area (m²) vs occupancy rate
- Classroom utilization rate
- Lab occupancy rate
- Disability accessibility compliance (% vs 60% norm)
- Equipment renewal backlog (count + DT value)
- Maintenance score per building
- Ongoing construction projects (% completion)

**Features:**
- Space management map
- Maintenance request system
- Project tracker

---

### 10. Équipements (Equipment)
**KPIs:**
- Total active equipment count
- Equipment age distribution
- % equipment requiring renewal
- IT equipment average age (flag >5 years)
- Budget coverage for renewal (% of need)

**Features:**
- Equipment registry with lifecycle tracking
- Renewal planning tool
- Procurement request workflow

---

### 11. Magasin / Inventaire (Inventory)
**KPIs:**
- Stock levels per category
- Consumption rate per department
- Stockout incidents per month
- Procurement cycle time

**Features:**
- Inventory management with low-stock alerts
- Supplier management
- Purchase order workflow

---

### 12. Formation Continue (Continuing Education / Training)
**KPIs:**
- Number of continuing education programs offered
- Enrollment in continuing education
- Revenue from continuing education
- Completion rate
- Employer satisfaction score

**Features:**
- Program catalog for continuing education
- Enrollment and billing
- Certificate generation

---

### 13. ESG / RSE (Environmental & Social Governance)
**KPIs:**
- Energy consumption (kWh/student/year)
- Carbon footprint (tCO2e)
- Renewable energy share (%)
- Recycling rate (%)
- Green mobility rate (% students using sustainable transport)
- Campus accessibility score

**Features:**
- Energy meter integrations
- ESG reporting dashboard
- Carbon offset tracking

---

### 14. Stratégie (Strategy)
**KPIs:**
- Strategic plan objectives completion rate
- KPI target achievement rate (vs annual goals)
- Institutional ranking trends
- Accreditation portfolio health

**Features:**
- Strategic plan builder (OKR-style)
- KPI target setting per institution
- Annual review report generator

---

### 15. Logistique (Logistics)
**KPIs:**
- Fleet utilization rate (vehicles)
- Transport request fulfillment rate
- Event logistics completion rate
- Supplier delivery performance

**Features:**
- Vehicle fleet management
- Transport request system
- Event logistics coordination
